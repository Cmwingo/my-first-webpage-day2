My Favorite Places

Leiden, The Netherlands

Leiden is a small village just outside of Amsterdam. The streets are lined with canals and filled with bicycle traffic. Walking along the canal in the early morning, the aroma of coffee and baked goods, and the crisp air is one of the best feelings in the world. Stop by Relaxed Coffeeshop while you're there and enjoy and esspresso, and maybe something more.

Megeve, France

Megeve is a small town located in the French province of Haute-Savoie. It's about a 45 min drive from the Geneva airport which makes it an easy destination for skiers, snowboarders and hikers alike. The charming town square offers many fine and casual dining options. Check out the Brasserie Centrale for a great traditional French meal.

London, England

London is the quintessential European city. Classic landmarks dot the city landscape. International travelers from numerous countries visit London each year. For a classic London pub experience try the Cross Keys Pub in Chelsea.
